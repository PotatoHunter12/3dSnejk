Goal for the game is to eat as many planets, asteroids and moons as possible. Snake grows as you eat more and more space objects.



Beware of satelites. If you happen to eat one, you lose the game and have to start over. Same thing happens if you reach the end of the map or collide into yourself.

To move the snake to the left, press key A.

To move the snake to the right, press key D.

To move the snake up, press key W.

To move the snake down, press key S.
